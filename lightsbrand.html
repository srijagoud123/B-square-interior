Lights Brands We Use

wipro Lighting
Wipro Lighting offers modern, energy-efficient LED and smart-connected lighting solutions suitable for homes, offices and large commercial spaces. Their product range spans downlights, panels, linear lighting and architectural systems designed for low energy use and long life. Wipro promotes a “Right Light” approach — matching light quality to the task and space to improve comfort and productivity. They also provide dealer locators and support for installations and warranties.

Havells (Lighting)
Havells is a well-known Indian electrical brand offering a broad interior lighting range — from designer home lights and downlighters to LED panels, battens and smart lighting options. Their fixtures emphasize durability, good lumen output and safety features (surge/high-voltage protection), making them popular for residential and commercial interiors. Havells also sells online and through an extensive dealer network, with plenty of product categories for decorative and functional lighting.
