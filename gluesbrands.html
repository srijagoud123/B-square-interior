Fevicol
Fevicol is India’s most recognized adhesive brand, widely used in woodworking, furniture manufacturing, and interior projects. It offers strong bonding for wood, laminates, veneers, MDF, and other interior materials. With products like Fevicol SH, Marine, and HeatX, it caters to various needs including water resistance and heat endurance. Fevicol’s easy application and non-toxic formulation make it safe for home and professional use. For decades, it has been the go-to choice for carpenters and interior professionals.
