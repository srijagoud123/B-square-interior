Plywood Brands We Use

Austin Plywood
Austin is a premium brand offering marine-grade plywood with excellent strength and surface finish. It is widely used in modular kitchens, wardrobes, and luxury furniture. Their plywood is moisture-resistant, warp-free, and long-lasting. Austin products are ISO and BIS certified for trusted quality. We use Austin for premium interiors where durability matters most.

Greenply
Greenply is one of India's most trusted and eco-friendly plywood manufacturers. Their plywood is termite- and borer-resistant, and comes with E0 emission standards. The brand offers MR, BWR, and fire-retardant boards for various uses. Their quality is backed by sustainable production practices. Ideal for modern, safe, and long-lasting interior projects.

CenturyPly
CenturyPly is known for its innovative Firewall and ViroKill technologies in plywood. They offer moisture-proof, anti-bacterial, and termite-resistant boards. Ideal for both residential and commercial spaces needing high durability. Their products meet international safety and quality standards. CenturyPly is our go-to brand for high-performance interior needs.

TESA Plywood
TESA is known for manufacturing calibrated plywood, blockboards, and flush doors. Their plywood is made with hardwood cores and is moisture-resistant and warp-free. Used widely in wardrobes, partitions, and modular furniture. ISO-certified, TESA products offer smooth finish and strength. We rely on TESA for quality, cost-effective interiors.
